Overnight Oats

Description

Overnight oats with tons of protein! Turned out the "rolled oats" you need for these are just the normal oats I buy for hot oatmeal, and the texture is better than hot oatmeal somehow!

Ingredients

  • Desired amount of oatmeal
  • Correct amount of oat milk and/or water for oatmeal (I always eyeball it so I can't help you here, sorry! Kinda dropping the ball on the whole recipe website thing here...)
  • Spoonful or two of yogurt
  • As much pea protein powder (or preferred protein powder) as you want
  • Tablespoon or two of chia seeds

Steps

  1. Combine oatmeal, oat milk and/or water, and chia seeds in tupperware the night before and let sit in regrigerator
  2. In morning, add yogurt and protein powder and mix in
  3. Could add fruit or other toppings too!
  4. Let dough sit for 5 minutes
